# Birthday Reveal — Roadmap & Feature Ideas

> Saved: May 26, 2026
> Repo: https://github.com/crowfly22/birthday-reveal
> Live: https://crowfly22.github.io/birthday-reveal/

---

## 🚀 Quick Wins (1-2 jam)

1. **Share Buttons** — WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter, Instagram Story share
2. **Custom Cover Image** — User upload foto orang yang ultah jadi background reveal
3. **Countdown Timer** — Set tanggal ultah, QR scan sebelum hari-H show countdown
4. **Save as Video/GIF** — Record canvas reveal jadi MP4/GIF (MediaRecorder API)
5. **More Themes** — Neon, Retro 80s, Cyberpunk, Winter, Tropical, Anime, Minimalist, Dark Luxury

## 💡 Medium Features (half day)

6. **Photo Wish Cards** — Setiap wish card bisa upload foto + text
7. **Multi-Page Reveal** — Intro → Photos → Wishes → Message → Cake (swipe/scroll)
8. **Custom Music** — Upload MP3 atau pilih preset playlist
9. **Collaborative Wishes** — Unique link, orang lain submit wishes → terkumpul jadi 1 QR
10. **QR Design Customization** — Warna custom, gradient, logo/foto di tengah QR

## 🎯 Big Features (1-3 hari)

11. **Template System** — Pre-built: "For Mom", "For Partner", "For Bestie", "For Kid", "For Coworker"
12. **AI Message Generator** — "Generate birthday message for my best friend who loves cats"
13. **Physical Card Integration** — Export QR + card design sebagai PDF printable (A5/A6)
14. **Analytics Dashboard** — Track scans, timestamps, country, device
15. **Multi-Language** — EN/ID/CN/JP/KR/AR/ES + RTL support

## 💰 Monetization

16. **Freemium Model** — Free: basic themes, 3 wishes, watermark | Pro ($3-5): all themes, 10 wishes, custom music, no watermark, analytics, video
17. **API / Embed** — Jual API buat florist, gift shop, event planner
18. **White Label** — Solution buat event organizer, wedding planner, HR tools

## 🏆 Viral Mechanics

19. **Gallery / Wall of Love** — Public gallery of birthday reveals (opt-in) — SEO content
20. **Streak / Engagement** — "You've created 5 birthday reveals this year!" share stats

---

## Priority Plan

- **FASE 1** → Share buttons + Save as video + More themes
- **FASE 2** → Collaborative wishes + Photo cards
- **FASE 3** → AI messages + Freemium model
